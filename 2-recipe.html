Chef John's Drunken Noodles

A flavorful Thai-inspired noodle dish packed with veggies and spice.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons cold water
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 (12 ounce) package dried rice noodles
  • 1/4 cup peanut oil
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup shallots, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups sliced bell peppers
  • 1 pound Chinese broccoli, sliced (Optional)
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • 1 cup fresh Thai basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce, or to taste

Directions

  1. Mix oyster sauce, soy sauce, fish sauce, maple syrup, sugar, sesame oil, water, and cornstarch in a bowl.

  2. Soak rice noodles in hot water until tender, drain and set aside.

  3. Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Cook eggs until scrambled, then set aside.
